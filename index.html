·About Us ·

Our History

We are Conundrum. A Draenor based horde guild who are actively recruiting for World of Warcraft: Shadowlands. Our history goes back into the end of Warlords of Draenor on the Genjuros server, where a new guild called Drifters would emerge to conquer all content that Legion had to offer. After clearing the first 2 raids and taking a brake Drifters got reorganized into Infinitus, this time changing realm to the Defias Brotherhood RP server. As Infinitus most of the current members met each other and raided, played together and even started having guild meet ups. Battle for Azeroth would bring us a new chapter in our lives when we had to reorganize again because of lack of players on the server and because the guild wanted to be so much more. We transfered to the Draenor server in an attempt to find new like minded players and because the ambitions of the guild had grown. Here it was where we cleared the first raids, teamed up for awesome mythic plus content and even killed a few bosses on Mythic. We are looking for players who know that World of Warcraft is about community. Players who have heard about some guilds meeting up, year after year, and think that forming such a friendship is something they want. We are looking for players who want to put in the effort for themselves but more so for their brothers in arms in game and friends in real life. If you are looking for a guild to make friends in, raid high end content, do high end mythic plus but also get to meet new friends along the way, consider joining!

Our Vision

We are grateful for the journey that brought us here but even more so we are excited to see what lies ahead. Most of us have played this game since day one and we, just like you, have noticed that most guilds have one focus. If they are doing too well in the raiding scene they probably aren't very friendly. If they are too friendly then that's a tell tale sign that it's no more but a glorified social guild. We have had our fair share of drama with this aswell. People are very different, some wanna raid mythic, others want to raid heroic, some opt to raid either mythic or heroic but not put in the effort for either of them relying on their guild mates to pick them up and carry most boss fights. In Shadowlands our vision is to create a guild that is truly capable of offering everything people log into the game for. Want to raid mythic? Sure, we raid 2-3 times a week and we would love to see you there! Want to raid Heroic content? That's great, join our HC only team and have fun! Want to do pet battles, fishing, PvP or any other activity the game has to offer? Awesome! We have a rank just for you so that it's easier to find guild members who share your same passions. All this cannot be accomplished without players and without the right mentality. If you are the type of person who knows that in order to get ahead you have to put in the work, you might be the person we're looking for. If you are willing to put in the effort for every activity you want to do then we would like to meet you. Help us create a one of a kind guild and be a part of our story.

· Rules ·

Always be prepared for the raid you want to be a part of. Do not expect to be carried. When people underperfom constantly it leads to frustration for the other people that are putting in the work.
Guild banter and trash talk will always happen. It's a sign of people becoming comfortable with one another. However if someone feels it's going to far and you are asked to stop: stop it.
Always be on time. We use the guild calendar to see what players we have for the activities we want to do. If you sign up and don't show up or show up and don't sign up, no spot is guaranteed. Ever.
Discord is a must for almost all activities. It's hard to create a community with just relying on writing in chat. When and if you can we strongly suggest you hang out in discord and mingle.
Constructive criticism is always appreciated. We all strive to be the best players we can be on our character and good advice is always welcomed, however never go to far with it when you see a person is actively trying.
Listening to advice is not the same as following it. We are all for hearing your thoughts on the way the guild is going however, if we don't go the direction you want that doesn't mean we are not listening, it means we don't agree with you.
Any member above the Trial rank can ask for a Guild Management meeting to express either positive or negative things. If one does so he will be summoned in the next officer meeting. If it's an emergency, one will be organized right away.
We believe in free speech. You can say whatever you want whenever you want but that doesn't mean you're free from consequences. Always use common sense, it gets you far.
We have a list of addons that are a must when you raid with us. These addons have proved to be indispensable over the years and we do check for them. The addons are: Deadly Boss Mods, Exorsus Raids tools, RC Loot council, and Weak Auras.
Make sure that all your gear is enchanted, socketed and that the flasks/pots you're using are the appropriate one for that patch. (Yes we did have a guy that was using flasks from a previous expansion cause: "hEy I hAvE fLaSks LiKe YoU sAid").
We require a mandatory 2 days attendace for raiding. Now let's define mandatory: for us it means show up for the content you signed up for when you joined. Life happens and we get that, but if you show up for 2 raids/month, we are not getting anywhere.
Our members are important to us. If you are the type of player that thinks you don't have to do X or Y with the guild because "it's fine they will just find a replacement", then we are not the guild you're looking for. We need players interested in community.

· F A Q ·

Q: When do you guys raid?

Our raid times and days are Wednesday and Monday from 7 Server time. In order for you to be elligible you must have signed up in the calendar.

Q: Can I also bring a friend to the guild?

Normally yes. If it's just a friend then we are always welcoming to new players, however if we are talking about 'large groups' such as when another guild is disbanding
then we will have an officer meeting to decide on our next move.

Q: When do you guys raid?

Our raid times and days are Wednesday and Monday from 7 Server time. In order for you to be elligible you must have signed up in the calendar.

Q: I usually don't fit in or don't want to talk. Is that ok?

Yes! That's absolutely no problem on our part, with the following mention: because most people can invoke this aspect of their personality all we ask is just stay on discord, muted and listen.
If after a few tries of this you still feel the same then by all means just be there for the content.

Q: How do you guys sort BoE's that might drop?

Usually high value BoE's end up in the guild bank as gold
after an auction or if one of our own players buys it at a discount. The reason for this is the fact that we provide flasks/food for every raid so that you can just enjoy the game.

Q: How do I join you guys?

Easily : if in the past you had to go through a lenghty 'application form' in order to be considered now all you have to do is check the bottom of the page
and click the discord icon. When you get there you will be able to contact either Willow directly or an officer and have a chat with him about joining. It's that easy.

Q: What if I have a problem with one of the guild members or management?

Any issue with our guild members should be brought to the immediate attention of one of the officers or the GM.
If the problem is with anyone from management, talk directly to the GM. If the issue is with the guild master then you can call all 3 officers for a chat in the GM's absence.

Q: What type of content do you guys normally raid?

At the start of an expansion we always clear the first normal raid to get a feel for it. After that we mainly focus on Heroic content until we can clear it in one go.
After that we through ourselves in the mythic raiding scene....Good luck to us.
